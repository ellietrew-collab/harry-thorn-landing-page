FAQs üí¨

How many days will I need to workout per week? +

This is totally up to you and what you can make work given your lifestyle, it can even be 0! This is because you don't actually need to workout to lose weight, so again, this all depends on you and your goals/prefences!

Do you make adjustments to nutrition and my workout plan? Or am i committing to one plan for the entire time? +

I will be making adjustments to your training and nutrition for the entire duration of our time together, this is all about creating an approach that truly works for you long-term, so edits are inevitable!

What if I have dietary restrictions? +

No problem! Your nutrition plan will be completely tailored to your preferences, allergies, and lifestyle.

Do I need to give up social events, eating out or alcohol? +

NO absolutely not, in my eyes, you're not healthy if you're not having fun! So again, this is all about making you a lifestyle that is realistic and enjoyable; so i factor in socialising, eating out and of course, alcohol too!

How does the money back guarantee work? +

I set you your goals when you sign up. These are designed to be precisely achievable in the time we are working together. If you work hard and do as I tell you, and do not achieve these goals, then I will give you money back (full refund) or work with you for free until you do.

Are they apps difficult to use? +

No they are super easy, have excellent support capabilities and I monitor you on both apps to take the workload off of you!

How long do you typically work with a client for? +

This depends on your goals, but over the last 5 years I have tended to work with clients for around 6 months, although I still have clients who started 5 years ago! So you can absolutely stay for a while, but you certainly don't need to. You will have all the tools you need to maintain your results after our initial time working together.
